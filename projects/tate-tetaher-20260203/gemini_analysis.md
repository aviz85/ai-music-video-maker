# MUSIC VIDEO STORYBOARD

## Theme
A high-energy, vibrant concert-style performance featuring warm stage lighting, capturing the celebratory and rhythmic essence of contemporary Hebrew pop-rock.

## Music Analysis
- **Genre:** Hebrew Pop-Rock / Mizrahi-infused Rock
- **Tempo:** ~128 BPM (Upbeat, driving)
- **Instruments heard:** Electric guitar (crunchy rhythm), Bass guitar, Drums (strong backbeat), Synth/Brass accents, Male Vocals.
- **Energy arc:** Starts with an energetic full-band intro, dips slightly for the lead-in line, then explodes into a rhythmic, anthemic chorus.
